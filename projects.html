E-commerce Profitability Analysis

Objective: Analyze factors affecting profit and build machine learning models to predict profitability.

What I did:

  • Cleaned and explored real-world e-commerce transaction data
  • Identified loss-making patterns caused by aggressive discounting
  • Performed feature engineering and trained regression models
  • Evaluated Linear Regression, Decision Tree, and Random Forest models

Key Insights:

  • High discounts (>40%) consistently resulted in negative profit
  • Technology products generated the highest average profit
  • Sales volume alone did not guarantee profitability

Tools: Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib
